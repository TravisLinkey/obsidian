## Overview

Replaces the traditional waterfall cycle with a repeating series of **speculate**, **collaborate**, and **learn** cycles.

Characteristics are:
    - Mission focused
    - Feature based
    - Iterative
    - Timeboxed
    - Risk Driven
    - Change Tolerant
